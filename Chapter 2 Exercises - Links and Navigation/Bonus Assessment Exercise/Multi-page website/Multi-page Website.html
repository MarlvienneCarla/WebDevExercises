Erica Saleh is the creator of the American teen drama mystery television series One of Us Is Lying.
The five high school kids who undergo detention are followed in the series, which is based on Karen M. McManus'
2017 novel of the same name. One of the students passes away under strange circumstances, and an investigation takes place.
